IIT GOA

IIT Goa, stands for the Indian Institute of Technology Goa, is one of
the newer IITs in India. It was established in 2016 and is located in the state of Goa.
The institute offers various undergraduate and postgraduate programs in engineering and technology.
With a focus on research and innovation, IIT Goa aims to provide quality education and contribute
to the development of the country.
